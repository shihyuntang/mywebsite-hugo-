### Description
This sequence is similar to the previous animation, but highlights the impact of incorporating magnetic fields (the Zeeman effect) into the model. Here, the SPIRou observations remain in black. The unmagnetized `EXSPLOSYN` forward model is plotted in red, while the updated `EXSPLOSYN` model incorporating a $2\text{ kG}$ magnetic field is shown in cyan.
