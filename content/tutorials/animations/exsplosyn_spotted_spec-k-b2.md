### Description
This animation demonstrates magnetic effects across two distinct K-band wavelength regions: panel (a) focuses on the highly magnetically sensitive Ti lines, while panel (b) showcases the magnetically insensitive CO molecular bands. In both panels, SPIRou observations are shown in black, the $2\text{ kG}$ `EXSPLOSYN` model is in red, and a $2\text{ kG}$ two-component composite spectrum built using `synmast` is represented in cyan.
